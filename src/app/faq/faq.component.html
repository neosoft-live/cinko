FAQ’s

  • 1. Is there a deposit to hold the room?

    Cinko does not charge a deposit; deposit rates are subject to change per hotel.

  • 2. Is there a deposit to protect against damage/incidentals to the room?

    This will be charged at the time of check-in by the hotel itself, not through Cinko.com. This is acknowledged in our Terms and Conditions on Cinko.com

  • 3. How are taxes and other fees collected by the hotel?

    This will be charged at the time of check-in by the hotel itself, not through Cinko.com. This is acknowledged in our Terms and Conditions on Cinko.com

  • 4. Is there a cancelation fee? If so, how much and will the full fee go directly to the hotel?

    • The customer is responsible for remitting the full amount of the hotel’s cancelation fee directly to the hotel. This is acknowledged in our terms and conditions on Cinko.com.

  • 5. Policy for double booking?

    • If the double booking was due to an error within Cinko.com, Cinko will be responsible to pay for the second guests’ room at full rate.
    • If the error was due to failure to remove a room or giving room to a non-Cinko guest, the hotel is responsible to accommodate the Cinko user in a room comparable at the Cinko rate.

  • 6. Are group bookings/room blocks available on Cinko?

    Not at this time, must contact hotel directly to inquire about room number, adjoining rooms, accessible rooms and so forth.

  • 7. Is there extended stay booking through Cinko?

    Not at this time, must contact hotel to inquire about multiple night stays. Pricing is subject to change after the agreed upon price paid for one night through Cinko.com.

  • 8. Does Cinko.com accept gift cards?

    Cinko.com does not accept or distribute gift cards currently.

  • 9. Can a customer change their booking information after the reservation has been made through Cinko.com?

    No, a customer cannot change their guest’s name, bed type, smoking preference, room type, number of guests, travel dates, etc. after the reservation has been confirmed through Cinko.com. It is upon the hotel’s discretion to allow any of the above change requests by the guest at the time of check-in.

  • 10. What if a customer reserves a room that is on Cinko as available, and the hotel does not have it available at the time of check-in due to double booking, not updating bookings that day, failure to delete booking, housekeeping concerns etc.

    It is the hotel’s responsibility to accommodate the customer with a comparable room at the Cinko rate.

  • 11. Can you use qualifying hotel points to pay for your Cinko room. As well as can you receive points with qualifying hotels booking through Cinko.com?

    • It is at the hotel’s digression if you will earn points for your stay.
