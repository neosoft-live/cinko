Cinko: When you do not make a reservation but need a hotel room at the last hour

About Us

In today's fast-paced world, we sometimes find ourselves someplace where we need to spend the night - but we did not plan to spend the night.

This is where Cinko helps tremendously! Using Cinko travelers are able to make a last-minute hotel reservation for that night's stay - at an amazingly inexpensive price!

Cinko is the perfect marriage for a traveler who unexpectedly needs a room for the night and a hotel who has a room that they thought would remain empty for the night!

How it works?

  • Download the free Cinko App.

  • Tell the app how far from where you are hoping to find a hotel room (1 mile, 2 miles, 5 miles).

  • Cinko finds a hotel near you which has an empty room for the night and matches you with that hotel.

  • Once Cinko matches you with the hotel, you will be charged for the room for that night (Usually USD 50*- which is why it is called Cinko!) and you receive instructions on where your hotel is located.
